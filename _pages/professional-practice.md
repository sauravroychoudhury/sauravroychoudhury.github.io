Beyond my academic role, I lead **SRC EvalMetrics LLC**, an economic impact and policy research consultancy based in Columbus, Ohio. SRC EvalMetrics helps public agencies, chambers of commerce, developers, nonprofits, universities, and policy stakeholders measure economic and fiscal impacts with transparent assumptions and practical reporting for boards, hearings, and policy decisions.

I bring rigorous economic modeling (IMPLAN, REMI, RIMS II), benefit-cost analysis, and applied econometrics to real-world questions, translating technical results into clear guidance for decision-makers.

## Services

- Economic and fiscal impact analysis
- Benefit-cost and return-on-investment studies
- Regional and industry economic modeling (IMPLAN, REMI, RIMS II)
- Policy research and program evaluation
- Expert analysis and reporting for boards, hearings, and policy decisions

## Selected Consulting & Policy Reports

**Economic Impact of Data Centers in Ohio (2025)**
Lead author of a comprehensive statewide study analyzing the employment, GDP, fiscal, and community impacts of data center development across multiple regions in Ohio.

**Columbus Metropolitan Housing Authority (CMHA) Economic Impact Study (2024)**
Led a detailed economic impact analysis of CMHA's public housing operations on Franklin County, the Columbus MSA, and the State of Ohio. Commissioned by the Ohio Chamber of Commerce Research Foundation.
