I teach across finance and economics at the undergraduate and graduate levels, with an emphasis on applied, hands-on learning using real data, financial modeling, and spreadsheet-based analysis.

## Courses Taught

**Capital University — Undergraduate:** Corporate Finance; Investment Finance; Financial Institutions, Markets & Risk; Financial Modeling; International Business; Money & Banking; Intermediate Macroeconomics & Money; Econometric Methods; Introduction to Political Economy.

**Capital University — MBA:** Financial Management; Financial Modeling; Analytic & Quantitative Methods (Data Analytics).

**Indian Statistical Institute (M.Sc. Quantitative Economics):** Finance Theory II.

**West Virginia University (Undergraduate):** Introduction to Finance; Survey of Finance; Business Finance; Financial Economics.

## Textbooks

**Modern Financial Management: Theory, Applications, and Managerial Decisions.** Activebook / Linus Learning, ISBN: 979-8-90050-036-2, 2026. Written for my MBA Financial Management course, this textbook integrates corporate finance theory with applied, spreadsheet-based managerial decision-making.

**Modern Investments** *(work in progress).* A forthcoming textbook covering investment theory, portfolio construction, asset pricing, and financial markets, taught with an applied, data-driven approach.
