My research lies at the intersection of financial economics, public policy, and regional economic development. I use rigorous empirical methods and economic modeling to examine how policy decisions, infrastructure investments, and market forces shape economic outcomes.

## Research Interests

- Financial Economics and Corporate Governance
- Public Policy Evaluation and Economic Freedom
- Leveraged ETFs, Volatility, and Financial Market Dynamics
- Innovation, Regulation, and Entrepreneurship
- Economic Impact Analysis and Regional Modeling (IMPLAN, REMI, and RIMS II)
- Applications of AI in Finance

## Selected Research Projects

**Economic Impact of Data Centers in Ohio (2025)**
Lead author of a comprehensive statewide study analyzing the employment, GDP, fiscal, and community impacts of data center development across multiple regions in Ohio.

**Columbus Metropolitan Housing Authority (CMHA) Economic Impact Study (2024)**
Led a detailed economic impact analysis of CMHA's public housing operations on Franklin County, the Columbus MSA, and the State of Ohio.
