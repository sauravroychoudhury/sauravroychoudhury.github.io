# Consulting

**SRC EvalMetrics LLC**  
**Principal**

I lead SRC EvalMetrics LLC, an economic consulting firm specializing in economic impact analysis and regional economic modeling. The firm provides rigorous, data-driven analysis for public policy, infrastructure, and housing initiatives using established input-output frameworks, including IMPLAN and REMI.

## Selected Projects

- **Economic Impact of Data Centers in Ohio (2025)** — Lead author of a comprehensive statewide study analyzing employment, GDP, and fiscal impacts of data center development across multiple regions of Ohio.

- **Columbus Metropolitan Housing Authority Economic Impact Study (2024)** — Led the economic impact analysis of CMHA’s public housing operations on Franklin County, the Columbus Metropolitan Statistical Area, and the State of Ohio.

The firm also prepares expert economic evaluation reports and letters supporting immigration petitions in finance and economics-related fields.
